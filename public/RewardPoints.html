Rewarded Skill Points

Act 1
  • +1 - Quest: Kill Nessus. Greece.
Act 2
  • +2 - Quest: "A Beast of Legend" - Wadjet Canyons.
  • +2 - Skill points from obtaining the Eye and the Staff.

The "Reward Attribute Points" selection will allow you to add in these points to your build.
